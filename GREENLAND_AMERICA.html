GREENLAND

Traveling to Greenland means exploring one of the planet's most precious polar treasures.
In fact, you may find yourself wondering why you haven't taken a Greenland trip sooner.


Qaqortoq

Located in the far south of Greenland, Qaqortoq is the second largest town in the country. It is a popular destination for whale watching during the spring season.



Greenland is one of the best places to see the northern lights

Seeing the northern lights, or aurora borealis, is a mystical experience. Visit Greenland between September and April, and you can find out for yourself.
One of the best places to see the northern lights in Greenland is Scoresby Sund, the largest fjord system on Earth.



Glaciers and icebergs are abundant in Greenland

 Among the most impressive things you can see in Greenland are its enormous ice formations. Be sure to take a quality camera capable of
panoramic shots, because you'll likely make good use of it in Greenland.
