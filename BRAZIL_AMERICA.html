What we love about the Amazon is that there is always an adventure. Anywhere you go, you’ll take a different method of transport between small boats to luxury ones or even kayaks.
Our favourite way to get out and enjoy the Amazon adventure is to use tandem kayaks


Iguaçu Falls Location

Paraná Highlights: The largest waterfall system in the world, shared between Brazil and Argentina. The falls feature over 275 individual waterfalls,
cascading a total of 813 meters down the face of the cliff.



Pantanal Location

Mato Grosso Highlights: One of the largest and most biodiverse wetlands in the world, home to various animal
species such as caimans, capybaras, and bird species like storks and herons.



The Forest

 Of course, the forest itself is one of the main reasons why people visit this amazing destination. The beauty of this site is something that you will
want to feast your eyes on and you won’t regret making the journey here. Accounting for 20% of the oxygen in the world, the number of trees in this huge forest is very impressive.
